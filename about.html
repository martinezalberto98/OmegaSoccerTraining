About San Diego Soccer Trainers (SDST)

San Diego Soccer Trainers (SDST) is a newly formed private soccer coaching company committed to developing young players across San Diego County. Our mission is to create an environment where kids aged 4 to 18 can either begin their soccer journey or take their technical and tactical abilities to the next level.

SDST was founded by two passionate and proven coaches — Coach Carlon Scott and Coach Alberto "Tito" Martinez. With years of experience both on and off the field, their unmatched love for the game fuels their training approach. They are dedicated to nurturing talent, building confidence, and helping every player reach their full potential.

From the scenic coastlines to North County, down to South County, SDST proudly serves players throughout San Diego. Whether you’re brand new to the game or aiming to sharpen your competitive edge, San Diego Soccer Trainers is the place for players to excel and grow as soccer athletes.

Join SDST today — where development, passion, and love for the game come together to create the next generation of players.
